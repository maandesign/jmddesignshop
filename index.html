Legacy Fitness

SECTOR: Gym / Physical Fitness Centre
TYPE OF WORK: Branding / Rebranding

CONTEXT

Legacy Fitness was the first gym in Rivière du Rempart, Mauritius. Since 2015, the gym has grown successfully and trained more than 200+ athletes. Legacy Fitness values ​​the health of its customers and has experienced coaches to advise and train those who are new to the world of fitness.

Legacy Fitness aims to be more visible to the public and inspire people to join the gym, exercise, improve their progress and stay healthy.

The brand was completely integrated, including research, positioning, architecture, brand definition and visual identity. The logo was designed in the form of vertical and horizontal line shapes to symbolise strength and progress. The colour combination of red and black represents strength and a feeling of taking up new challenges.
